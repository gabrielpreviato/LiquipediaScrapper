Copa América 2018 Season 2 is the second season of Copa América 2018 . 16 players from Latin America battle it our for a $10,000 prize pool and two seeds to 2018 WCS Valencia .

Format [ edit ]

  • Group Stage
    • Four groups consisting of four players each, groups are played in a dual tournament format where all matches are Bo3.
    • 1st and 2nd place of each group advances to the next stage.
  • Playoffs
    • Double elimination bracket.
    • Winner's bracket and Loser's bracket round 3 and 4 are Bo5.
    • Loser's bracket round 1 and 2 are Bo3
    • Finals are Bo7, with the player coming from the winner's bracket starting with a 1 game advantage.
    • Top 2 are seeded into 2018 WCS Valencia .
